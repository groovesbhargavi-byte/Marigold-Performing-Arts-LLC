About

Marigold Performing Arts is devoted to nurturing excellence in Indian classical traditions. Rooted in discipline, depth, and cultural integrity, the academy offers immersive training in dance and music while cultivating artistic expression and confidence.

Founded with a vision to create meaningful artistic experiences, Marigold Performing Arts bridges heritage and contemporary presentation, bringing Indian classical traditions to a global stage.
